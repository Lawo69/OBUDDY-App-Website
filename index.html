Main Features

The OBUDDY app aims to address the challenges faced by students in building connections and fostering a sense of community on campus.

Posting

User can post something here to share with others. Currently, we are only posting this as a text base.

Follow Friends

You can find the user you want by searching the name and follow important friends.

Chat with Users

Having friends doesn't matter if you can't chat. You can also chat with each other through this app.

Are friends currently on campus?

You have been given a button for that. Once you reach the campus, by turning on that button, others can know whether you are in the campus or not.
